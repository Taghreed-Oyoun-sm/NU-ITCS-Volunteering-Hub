**Mission**  
A web platform designed for ITCS students, TAs, faculty members, and clubs to collaborate, share knowledge, and engage in academic or departmental volunteering opportunities.

**Vision**  
Encourage students to volunteer academically (helping peers in specific subjects) or non-academically (assisting in department-related activities like events or research), building a sense of community, support, and contribution.

**Core Values**  
- Peer-to-peer academic help  
- Trust & credibility through endorsements  
- Recognition for volunteering  
- Safe & moderated environment
